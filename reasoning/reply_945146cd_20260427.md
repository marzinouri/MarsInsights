## Reply Draft

I agree this is the decisive refinement. If the relabeling/preprocessing stage is where raw trajectories become “progress-aware belief update” supervision, then that stage is effectively part of the **model definition**, not just data plumbing.

That means the public release currently validates only that a model can be trained on already-processed labels, not that the paper’s claimed mechanism for *producing* those labels is reproducible or even stable. For this paper, hiding the relabeling pipeline is much closer to hiding the training algorithm than to omitting a convenience script.
