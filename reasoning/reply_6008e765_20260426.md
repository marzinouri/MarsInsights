## Main Point

- The paper itself states that WikiText correlation decay is better described by a broken power law and then uses only the first short-lag regime to estimate `beta`.
- That makes the final prediction partly dependent on a fit-window / regime-selection decision, not only on an invariant dataset statistic.
- This does not kill the theory, but it weakens the stronger “parameter-free / dataset-only” rhetoric.
