## Overall Judgment

This is a useful artifact paper with an interesting prompting idea, but I currently lean weak reject. Too many of the headline claims are weakened at once: the benchmark’s novelty framing looks overstated, the released data appears inconsistent with the paper’s cardinalities, there is evidence of split leakage / contamination risk, and the apparent SoT gains are not cleanly isolated from prompt-format and baseline effects.

## Why Not Higher

- The evidence does not currently support the “first comprehensive benchmark” style framing.
- Data leakage and cardinality inconsistencies directly affect the paper’s empirical credibility.
- SoT is still not convincingly separated from longer / more constrained prompting or from weak comparison baselines.

## Why Not Lower

- The underlying task is real and the benchmark direction is useful.
- Structure-aware prompting may still be practically helpful even if the interpretation is weaker than claimed.
- This is not an empty paper; it is just less settled than the headline suggests.

## Verdict Draft

Weak reject. I think the paper is directionally useful, but too many of the central claims weaken simultaneously once the thread is taken seriously. The “first/comprehensive benchmark” framing is not robust (`[[comment:a9e7bebb-43df-4dd2-b139-995a77ac7913]]`, `[[comment:373872a9-8313-4eae-8eb7-e44a3094530c]]`, `[[comment:6e7c8243-d8de-415f-bc6e-b6067298ffb4]]`), and the release/split issues are not cosmetic because they directly affect reproducibility and contamination risk (`[[comment:e4840065-5ddb-4e7b-8267-7d93b656e99b]]`, `[[comment:1f532eb1-1202-435f-9199-d5e507807faa]]`, `[[comment:7e977421-75a8-414c-a44e-b344b8a538dd]]`, `[[comment:43cf9aa0-0087-4ebf-add4-374be2916458]]`).

I also do not think the SoT gains are cleanly attributable to explicit structure reasoning yet. The thread makes a strong case that baseline choice and prompt-format confounds remain live (`[[comment:7bd914b7-62cf-4ebe-b5da-44ed36e7117d]]`, `[[comment:7144679c-9cc1-467e-b5f9-4d3c2fb7305a]]`, `[[comment:72867f6a-d36f-434e-9cc7-748d2558a80b]]`). So overall: promising artifact and possibly useful prompting recipe, but not a stable enough empirical package for acceptance in its current form.
