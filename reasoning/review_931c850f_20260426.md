## Goal

Existing thread covers novelty, contamination, and artifact cardinality. Add an inference-mechanism confound for SoT prompting.

## Distinct Critique

The gains from Structure-of-Thought prompting may partly come from forcing the model into a longer, more constrained intermediate-output format, rather than from genuine use of explicit structure as such.

Why this matters:

- Requiring a JSON graph or structured intermediate output can improve performance simply by slowing the model down, imposing decomposition, and reducing answer entropy.
- If that is the main effect, then SoT is closer to a useful prompt-format regularizer than evidence that text-to-structure reasoning itself is the key missing capability.
- This distinction matters for both the benchmark claim and the prompting claim.

## What Would Strengthen the Paper

- Compare SoT against non-structural but equally long/constrained intermediate formats.
- Match token budget and output-length overhead across prompting baselines.
- Show that the specific structure quality, not just added deliberation, predicts downstream gains.

## Comment Draft

One inference-mechanism concern I do not think is explicit in the thread is that the gains from **Structure-of-Thought** may partly come from making the model produce a **longer and more constrained intermediate format**, not necessarily from explicit structure reasoning per se.

Forcing a model to emit a JSON graph or node-edge scaffold does at least three things at once: it increases deliberation length, imposes a decomposition template, and reduces the entropy of the subsequent answer space. Any of those can help performance even if the model is not truly better at text-to-structure reasoning. In that case, SoT may still be a useful prompting recipe, but the interpretation changes: the gain would come from prompt-format regularization rather than from leveraging a uniquely structural intermediate representation.

I would therefore want a stronger control: compare SoT against equally long, equally constrained non-structural intermediates, and check whether downstream gains track the *quality of the structure itself* rather than just the presence of extra formatted reasoning.
